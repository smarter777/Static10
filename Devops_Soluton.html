Solution: DevOps Wizard
A successful DevOps strategy goes beyond automation to positively impact the entire software development lifecycle. Having a fully optimized DevOps approach helps engineering leaders and their organizations get the most out of the cloud.

The DevOps Wizard is the result of many years of experience building Continuous Integration and Continuous Delivery (CI/CD) pipelines for Startups and large corporations that require a scalable DevOps solution that follows best practices, at a fraction of the cost of hiring a DevOps team.

Our DevOps Wizard accelerates the development process by quickly implementing a proven and customizable solution for building and delivering your applications, thus freeing your software engineers to do what they do best, build great software.

DevOps Assessment & Strategy

Our team will devise a set of recommendations and roadmap that includes Continuous Integration best practices, version control management strategy, Continuous Delivery pipelines, and quality control automation.

CI/CD Automation

Automation of Continuous Integration and Continuous Delivery processes enables your team to quickly build and deploy your applications. Stratus10's team will provide the DevOps automation needed to help you speed up every step of your application build and deployment process.

Infrastructure as Code

Stratus10 will build the infrastructure for the CI/CD pipeline using code so your DevOps process can be managed via code which will make it easier to reproduce and track changes.

The DevOps Wizard will allow you to confidently deliver more frequent updates to your application while minimizing bugs and issues with your code. Our team will customize the solution to implement a Continuous Integration and Continuous Delivery (CI/CD) process to enable your team to quickly build and deploy your applications.

The DevOps Wizard will provide you with a quick-start, base-level, deployment process for up to three (3) environments for one (1) application.

Our team will devise a set of recommendations and roadmap that includes Continuous Integration best practices, version control management strategy, and Continuous Delivery pipelines.

 

* The solution includes basic deployment process for up to 3 environments. Additional features or environments can be requested at an additional cost.

  1. Requirements Gathering
    Stratus10 works with your team to identify and understand the current state of your AWS accounts, infrastructure, and delivery process.
     
  2. Solution Customization
    Stratus10 will customize the DevOps Starter Kit to meet your application requirements.
     
  3. Working Session
    Stratus10 may require additional meetings with your engineering team to make sure the solution is aligned to your needs. 
     
  4. Testing
    Stratus10 will deploy and test the solution on your environment.
     
  5. Documentation and Training
    Stratus10 will provide detailed documentation on the solution customized for your deployment use case and work with your team to ensure they understand how to maintain the solution moving forward.
